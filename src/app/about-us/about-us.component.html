Education Is The Process Of Facilitating Learning

The establishment of Mount Basil High School, Jadcherla saw the light of the day in 2016 with a resilient dictum that education is to be imparted in a holistic fashion. Mount Basil High School which is run under the aegis of Victory Educational Society has been the impetus for the accomplishments of our progenies. The holistic approach employed in MOUNT BASIL HIGH SCHOOL, propels our posterities stand the test of time. We strive to kindle the outlook in its stakeholders to embrace the change and brace for future challenges. In this sophisticated era where scholastic excellence draws the attention, we ensure that our children grow up to be the embodiment of all the required qualities to explore new horizons.

MOUNT BASIL HIGH SCHOOL which is situated in its sprawling 5.5 acres lush green campus with floral plants, colossal playground is sight to behold. This is the fourth off spring of victory educational society. In a span of few years, MOUNT BASIL HIGH SCHOOL, Jadcherla under the visionary leadership of the eminent management has witnessed noteworthy change in its stature.

Our Vision :

We are an institution of wholesome education excellence, imparting values and leading our students to build strong character and intellectual competence, to emerge torch bearers in all walks of life on global stage.

Our Mission :

To achieve our vision, we will prepare our students to become independent learners with the desire, the skills and the abilities necessary for lifelong learning. This will require creating a learning environment which is centred around students, directed by teacher.

Philosophy :

Our philosophy is based on “The Whole Child” concept, which is a framework for the development of four key areas that are essential for shaping a balanced and all-rounded personality. Our holistic system of learning sets quality benchmarks that prepare our students for success in the volatile modern world.
