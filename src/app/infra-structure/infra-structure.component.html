Maths Lab : In line with the philosophy of experiential learning, MOUNT BASIL HIGH SCHOOL provides a well equipped mathematics lab to enhance the understanding of the subject by practical means.

Science lab : The school has well – designed separate science labs for physics, Chemistry and Biology labs, which are fully equipped as per the guidelines laid by CBSE.

Infirmary : The school has an infirmary for sick students managed round the clock by a trained nurse. We are associated with S.V.S hospital for regular and emergency medical services.

Transportation : Efficiently navigated bus routes ensure that children spend the least time commuting to school. Every bus is procured as per the guidelines of government of Telangana state Transport Department. Every bus is stacked with first aid kit and speed governor. Besides, a support staff accompanies the children in every trip. Assigned staff member is trained to address the contingency and ensure the safety and security of the children under his / her care.
